    Project Overview

    The Spelman College Innovation Lab is transitioning from the College's science center to the newly build Mary Schmidt Campbell Center for Innovation and the Arts. With the new location, the lab needs new branding and digital materials. The team: Jaycee Holmes, Madison Metivier, Haniyah Hakim, and Sydney Dixon (Student designers, professionals, and college faculty) worked together to create Instagram posts, email banners, and research day posters to modernize the lab and its brand.

    Nutrihealth

    Project Overview

    During the summer of 2024, I participated in an internship called TECC BOSS. During this program, I was able to work with a team of students like myself to solve problems in our neighboring communities using technology and design. Our Team included, designers, project managers, developers, and researcher: Haniyah Hakim, Morgan Lincoln, Joshua Caskey, and Jacobi Wilson.

    Low income communities struggle with foodborne conditions, such as low blood pressure, diabetes, and malnutrition. Determine how to combat these issues of malnutrition for low income communities, and tackle the lack of education and resources provided in these areas.
